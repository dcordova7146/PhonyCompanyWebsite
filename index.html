Example Zones in Sonic Superstars

Bridge Island zone

Located at the southmost tip of the Northstar Islands where lush greenery meets the open sea. Race at high speeds and jump to propel yourself through the air with ease. From running on walls to dashing through loops, this zone is packed with classic sonic fun… Just watch out for the Mosasaurus-like giant robot roaming around!

Frozen Base Zone

Frozen Base Zone is the tenth and penultimate Zone of Sonic Superstars. It is an underground, frozen stronghold on the Northstar Islands belonging to Dr. Eggman. The Zone contains two standard Acts, as well as an optional Act exclusive to Tails.

Golden Capital Zone

Golden Capital Zone is the eighth Zone of Sonic Superstars. It is a location on the Northstar Islands. The Zone contains two standard Acts, as well as an optional Act exclusive to Knuckles.

Lagoon City Zone

Lagoon City Zone is the fifth Zone of Sonic Superstars. It is a ruined, submerged city in the Northstar Islands. It is composed of two Acts, plus an exclusive Act for Amy.

Pinball Carnival Zone

A bustling park where you can enjoy colorful neon lights and fireworks. By day, it’s a vibrant world for thrill-seekers, but by night, the gloomy atmosphere creeps in... There are plenty of unique and fun Sonic attractions like pinball machines and jet coasters! At the end of the zone, a giant Dr. Eggman lies in wait
